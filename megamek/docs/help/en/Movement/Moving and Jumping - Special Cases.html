Jumping into Buildings

Any unit capable of jumping can jump onto a bridge, the jump will default to landing on top of the building, this is the intended behavior, and you cannot jump into the lower floors if you are not jumping into one of the side of the building.

Jumping into Bridges

Any unit capable of jumping can jump onto a bridge, the jump will default to landing on top of the bridge, however if you want to land on terra-firma instead, you can click the "go down", if the MP cost shows up as grey it means you moved down to a level that has no ground, you can keep clicking the go down button until you touch the hex floor. a WiGE can be settled 1 elevation above ground, and hover vehicles can be settled on the water level.

Moving 0MP Infantry

Infantry with 0MP can still move normaly on the field, however one thing needs to be taken care of, they can't climb more than 1 level at a time, so to enter a building you need to change the climb mode to "Go Through", this will allow them to get inside the building instead of trying to climb it up.

Once inside the building, the 0MP infantry can use Go Up and Go Down to move between floors.

Moving WiGE

If you are moving a WiGE on a field with some minor changes in height going down, you need to, in each hex where the level decreases, use the "go down" command, otherwise it will try to keep the elevation, which cost a higher MP.
