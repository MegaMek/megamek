Laying Mines

The action "Lay mines" can only be done in the Movement Phase. The Fa Shih can lay mines at the beginning or end of its movement, depending on the order of actions taken.

This describes how to use the Fa Shih to lay mines, it only works with those who carry mines.

Mines in the starting hex

  • Declare jumping mode,
  • Click Lay Mines,
  • In the dialog asking for which Trooper to lay mine you wil select one and hit Okay,
  • Click on the hex to which you want the unit to jump into,

Mines in the final hex

  • Declare jumping mode,
  • Click on the hex to which you want the unit to jump into,
  • Click Lay Mines,
  • In the dialog asking for which Trooper to lay mine you wil select one and hit Okay,

Illustrated Example: Mines in the starting hex

After declaring jumping mode, the "Lay Mines" button will be enabled

Clicking on Lay Mines a dialog will show up asking which trooper you want to choose.

After confirming the dialog, you select the hex into which you want to land the jumping infantry.

The mines will show up in the hex from which the Fa Shih jumped from.
